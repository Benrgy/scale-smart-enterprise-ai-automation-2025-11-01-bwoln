About Scale Smart

Founded in 2018 by a team of AI researchers and enterprise technology veterans, Scale Smart emerged from a simple observation: most organizations were struggling to harness the power of artificial intelligence due to complexity, cost, and lack of expertise. We built our platform to democratize enterprise AI, making advanced automation accessible to organizations of all sizes. Over the past six years, we've grown from a startup to a trusted partner for Fortune 500 companies, helping them automate billions of transactions and unlock unprecedented operational efficiency.

Our mission is to empower enterprises to thrive in the age of AI by providing intelligent, secure, and scalable automation solutions. We believe that technology should serve people, not replace them. That's why we've built our platform with a human-centric approach, ensuring that AI augments human capability and enables teams to focus on what matters most. Our core values—innovation, integrity, and impact—guide every decision we make, from product development to customer success.
